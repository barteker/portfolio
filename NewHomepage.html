Bart Baker

Hi! My name is Bart Baker.

I'm a musician, designer, athlete and engineer who wants to take friction out of life, giving beauty to small tasks.

Born and raised in Menlo Park, CA, I have always been a creative and a problem solver. This led me to enroll at CU Boulder pursuing a dual degree in Piano Performance as well as one in Creative Technology and Design.

I love making things that look, feel, and sound intentional; now I want to help others broadcast their passions in a clean cohesive and concise manner. If I can't make your vision come true, I know someone who can. Either way, I want to help you figure it out!
